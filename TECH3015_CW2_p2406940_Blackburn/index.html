About Us

ALEXIA Designs, founded by Charli Blackburn in 2019 at the age of 21, has become a multi-award winning Southern California based interior design company. ALEXIA is known for their broad range of styles and unique, yet sophisticated interiors. Still new to the game but, ALEXIA has already found themselves to be featured in Architectural Digest, and Elle Decor, along with other magazines.
